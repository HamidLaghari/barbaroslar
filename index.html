Barbaroslar

Ottoman supremacy in the Mediterranean

Barbaroslar is an upcoming Turkish television series directed by The Taylan Brothers and written by Cüneyt Aysan, Ozan Aksungur and Oğuz Ayaz. It is a historical non-fiction series that was begun shooting in the last weeks of March 2021. It stars Engin Altan Düzyatan and Ulaş Tuna Astepe with the lead roles. The series describes the life of Barbaros Hayreddin Pasha, a "Kapudan Pasha" of the Ottoman Empire.
